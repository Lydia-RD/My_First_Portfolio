Astronomy: The Gateway to the Stars

Astronomy allows us to explore the vastness of the universe from our backyards and learn how everything fits together.

With just a small telescope or binoculars you can observe planets, stars, and the phases of the moon.

Begin with a simple star chart and learn constellations — that will make the sky much less mysterious.

There are many online guides and communities that provide observing tips and project ideas.
